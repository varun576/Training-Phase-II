Munnar

Welcome to Munnar! This place is known for its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. Whether you are an adventurer, a nature lover, or someone who simply wants to relax, this destination has something to offer for everyone.

Key Attractions

  • Tea Gardens: Munnar is famous for its vast tea plantations that stretch across rolling hills. Visitors can take a leisurely walk through the tea gardens, witness the tea-picking process, and enjoy breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Mattupetty Dam: This scenic dam is located about 13 kilometers from Munnar and offers a picturesque view of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Visitors can enjoy boating on the serene Mattupetty Lake.
  • Eravikulam National Park: This national park is home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, a rare mountain goat species. The park is known for its stunning landscapes and a variety of flora and fauna.
  • Anamudi Peak: Situated in the Eravikulam National Park, Anamudi Peak is the highest peak in South India. Trekkers and nature enthusiasts can enjoy challenging treks to the summit for breathtaking views.
  • Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary: Located at a distance from Munnar, this sanctuary is a great place for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. It's home to various species of animals and birds
